Bachelor's degree in Applied Computer Science

The professional Bachelor of Applied Computer Science offers a professional and practice-oriented training program by teaching professional, multifunctional knowledge and competences. Working from four perspectives: Application development, Systems and Networks, Business and Problem Solving. The bachelor trains students to become IT professionals with a strong technological background, strongly embedded in the business context, generically as well as specialized by majors.
